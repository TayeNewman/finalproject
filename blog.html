Why You Need A Social Media Command Center The emergence of the social media command centers is helping brands and companies manage their social campaigns. Login to one central hub to access all the tools needed to execute, track and measure social media programs. Eight Reasons why command centers are a better way to run social campaigns. Listen Get to know your market. Understand what the community cares about. Engage Likes, Comments, Shares etc. Chat when it makes sense. A simple thank you can speak volumes about the nature of a brand.Community - Work with your team to build a community of friends, fans and followers. Turn Strangers into fans. Turn fans into customers.Brand Advocacy Turn customers into brand advocates. The Internet enables customers to speak on behalf of your brand without being asked to do. These social conversation are the new digital word of mouth. Customer recommendations are more valuable than recommendation of social influencers paid to tweet. Content Build real-time content based on the news cycle. What is happening today? How can my brand be involved? Innovation Customer feedback plus the collective intellect of community can result in product innovations. Lead Generation Track real-time users who are searching for what you have. Research Data. “if you’re not measuring, you’re not marketing” There is a tonne of data available to marketers. The challenge is sifting through it to find the nuggets of actionable insights. Support When looking for customer service, more and more customers are reaching out to a company branded social media platform … Quick response to customer issues is expected. Command Centers make escalations manageable.
